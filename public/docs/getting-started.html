Getting started

Create a room

Hit Create a room on the home page, or just visit any room URL you like — /room/your-team-name — and it's yours to claim. Room names are lowercase letters, digits, and dashes.

When creating from the home page you can pick a starting preset:

  • 🏃 Sprint planning — Fibonacci deck, all features on (the default)
  • 👕 T-shirt sizing — XS–XL deck
  • 🚑 Triage — grouped severity tiers (Urgent/High/Medium/Low), no timer chimes or vote stats
  • 🎯 Minimal — just cards and a reveal; queue, history, stats, countdown, and chimes all off

Presets only seed the room; everything stays editable in room settings.

Invite the team

The room URL is the invite — copy it from the "Invite your team" panel. Everyone enters a name and joins as a voter or an observer (observers watch and manage, but don't hold up the reveal).

Vote and reveal

  1. Type what you're estimating into the story description (it syncs live).
  2. Voters pick a card. Others see that you've voted, never what.
  3. When every connected voter has voted, cards flip automatically (auto-reveal is a setting; there's also a Show votes button and an optional ⏳ countdown that reveals at zero).
  4. Next ticket → records the round to history and pulls the next story from the queue; Re-vote re-runs the same story without recording.

Seats, refreshes, and hosts

  • Refreshing the page reclaims your seat — name, role, and current vote.
  • Leave room frees your seat so it doesn't block auto-reveal.
  • The first joiner is the host (⚙️ room settings, 👑 host transfer). If the host disconnects, the longest-seated member is promoted; the original host reclaims the role on return unless they handed it off explicitly.
  • Rooms keep settings and history for months between sessions, and clean themselves up after ~60 days of no use at all.
